About Us

The independent craft workshop “Akustika” from Ovčar Banja was founded on June 1, 1986. Since then, we have developed in line with market demands, listening to the needs of our clients and continuously improving our technology and expertise.

Our production program includes a wide range of services:

  • Production of electromechanical components according to catalog or customer documentation
  • Cutting all types of openings in enclosures according to customer requirements
  • Powder coating based on customer requests
  • Production of all types of springs from wire with diameters ranging from 0.2 to 12 mm (spring steel wire according to DIN 17223 and stainless steel wire according to DIN 17224 and 17225)
  • Production of housings for poker machines based on request, documentation, or sample provided by the customer

Elements are made from the following materials:
Č.0147, Č.4580, stainless steel, and aluminum sheet metal.

Our goal is to deliver maximum quality and precision in every product, respecting deadlines and agreements with our clients.
